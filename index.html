Here's the formula this calc was built with. I did some research and found this one to work pretty well. I always round my spokes up by 2mm, just to ensure a bit more strenth at the eyelet.

l is the spoke length

x is the number of crosses
h is the total number of spokes in the wheel
dh is the diameter of the spoke hole
